About

Referral Link Generator is the simplest way to add a Stripe-native referral program to your SaaS. No dashboards to learn, no revenue-sharing middlemen — just referral links that work.

Built for indie hackers and early-stage SaaS founders. Embed in 2 lines of HTML. Your Stripe account, your data, your domain.

What it does

Referral links — Generate unique referral codes. Friends get a Stripe discount. Referrers earn credit. Conversion tracking via webhooks.
G2 / Capterra reviews — Track user activity, detect happy users, and automatically send review requests when they're most likely to convert.

Pricing

Starter: Free (up to $500/mo referral revenue + 10 reviews/mo). Pro: $29/mo (no limits). No percentage of your revenue, no seat fees.

Open source

MIT licensed. GitHub. Self-host with Docker, or use the hosted version at reflinkgen.com.
