Common questions

What counts as "referral revenue"?

Revenue attributed to a referral link — when a referred customer completes a Stripe checkout using the promo code generated for that link. Only successful, paid conversions count. Trials, failed payments, and refunds are excluded.

Do I pay 5% on all my Stripe revenue?

No. Only on revenue from customers who came through a referral link and used the promo code. Your direct/organic customers are 100% yours — no platform fee ever.

What happens at exactly $1,000 referral revenue?

The 5% fee kicks in on revenue after the first $1,000. You don''''t pay retroactively on the first $1K — it''''s a threshold, not a cliff. The dashboard shows your running total so there''''s never a surprise.

Can I self-host and avoid the fee entirely?

The code is open source (MIT) — you can self-host the open-source version forever without any platform fee. The fee applies if you use our hosted service or want the Pro features (priority support, white-label, SLA, advanced analytics). Either way, you own your data.

How do referrers get paid?

You control the reward amount (default $10 per conversion) in REWARD_AMOUNT. The system tracks each referrer''''s balance in the reward_balance column. Payouts are manual — you decide when/how to pay (account credit, PayPal, bank transfer, gift cards). No automatic money movement by our system.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime. If self-hosted, just stop the container — your data stays in your SQLite file. If hosted, export your data (CSV/SQLite) and we''''ll delete your account. No contracts, no lock-in, no "enterprise sales call" required to leave.
