How it works

Three steps from link to paid referral — all handled by your Stripe account.

Generate link

Customer visits your app → widget calls POST /api/links → creates referrer + unique code REF-A1B2C3D4 + Stripe promo code.

Friend clicks & checks out

Link /ref/A1B2C3D4 records click → redirects to your Stripe Checkout with ?promo=REF-A1B2C3D4 → friend gets 20% off.

You get paid, referrer gets credit

Stripe webhook checkout.session.completed fires → matches promo → marks conversion → credits referrer $10 balance.

Frequently asked questions

What is Referral Link Generator?
A Stripe-native referral engine that lets you add a referral program to your SaaS in 2 lines of HTML. It also collects G2/Capterra reviews automatically.
How does Stripe integration work?
The service uses your Stripe account to create coupons and promo codes automatically. A webhook listens for checkout.session.completed and credits the referrer.
Can I self-host?
Yes. The project is MIT licensed and ships with a Docker image. Deploy to Railway, Render, Fly.io, or your own VPS.
Do you take a percentage of my referral revenue?
No. We charge flat monthly pricing. No revenue share, no seat fees, no percentage of anything.
How long does it take to set up?
About 2 minutes. Drop the embed script, initialize with your user's email, and the widget renders instantly.
